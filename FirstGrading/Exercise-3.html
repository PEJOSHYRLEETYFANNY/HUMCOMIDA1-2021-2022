Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic, minced chopped
  • 1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger,
    peeled and thinly sliced
  • tablespoon fish sauce
  • 2 pound chicken legs and thighs, rinsed
    and patted dry
  • (10 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 2 chayote squash, peeled and cut
    into bite-size pieces
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 galloon ketchup
  • 1 head bok choy, chopped
  • 1/2 pound spinach

Preparation

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over mdeium heat;
  2. cook and stir the oinio and garlic in the hot oil until it fragrant.
  3. Quickly stir the ginger and fish sauce into the oinion and garlic mixture before adding the chicken
  4. Cook together for 5 minutes
  5. Pour the chicken broth ober the mixture and cook another 5 minutes
  6. Add the chayote to the mixture and stimmer until the chicken is no longer pink in the centr, wait in 10 minutes more.
  7. Season with salt and pepper
  8. Add the cok choy and spinach
  9. Boil the pasta in 15 minutes
  10. Cook until the spinach is just wilted, 1 to 2 minutes. Serve hot,
